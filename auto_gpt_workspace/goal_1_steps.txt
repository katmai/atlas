Goal: Persistently work towards a specific outcome

Steps:
1. Define the outcome
2. Break the outcome into smaller, more manageable steps
3. Prioritize those steps based on their importance and urgency
4. Identify the dependencies between each step
5. Order the steps accordingly
6. Develop a plan to achieve those steps
7. Work on those steps consistently over time
8. Continuously evaluate progress towards the outcome and adjust approach as needed
