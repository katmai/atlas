Qualities that make a river unique:
- Flows downhill from the force of gravity
- Can be wide or shallow
- May flow year-round or only during certain seasons
- Has a starting point and an endpoint where water empties into a larger body of water
- Supports plant and animal life
- Can form geologic wonders
- Used by humans for various purposes such as irrigation, drinking water, transportation, and leisure activities

Reflecting on these qualities, I can embody the qualities of a river by:
- Going with the flow
- Adapting to changing circumstances
- Supporting life
- Being a source of wonder and inspiration
- Being useful to others
