A guide on how to make a cake from scratch, including tips on choosing a recipe, preparing baking pans, allowing ingredients to reach room temperature, preheating the oven, stirring together dry ingredients, creaming butter and sugar, adding eggs one at a time, alternating adding dry and wet ingredients, pouring batter into pans and baking, checking for doneness, cooling cake layers, assembling the cake, adding the first coat of frosting, and frosting and decorating the cake.
