The Deer Glen nature reserve and educational trail in Brno, Czech Republic is 3 kilometers long with 6 ecologically focused stations. The trail is located in the picturesque region of the Podkomorské Forests and starts from Kozí horka by the Brno Reservoir. The nature reserve has 167 plant species, 26 bird species, 16 moss species, and 11 wood-decay fungus species. The trail can be accessed by public transport.
